Prizes

A. The Rector's Prizes

1.an award to the student with highest GPA overall amongst the graduands in the master of official statistics. the prize will be a crystal prize, certificate, and Tsh. 300,000.

2.An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands in the Bachelor of official Statistics, Bachelor of data science, Bachelor of agriculture statistics and Bachelor of Business statistics. the prize will be crystal prize, certificate, and Tsh.250,000.

3.An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands the ordinary Diploma in statistics. the prize will be a crystal prize, certificate, and Tsh. 200,000.

4.An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands in the Technician Certificate in Statistics and IT. the prize will be a crystal prize, Certificate, and Tsh. 100,000.

5.An award to the male and female academic staff for Research and publications. the award is to; 1. first Authorship:the degree to which the nominee served as the primary contributor to the research; 2. peer review journal-Double blinded: confirmation that the publication underwent a rigorous double -blinded peer review; 3. Publication year -2021/22, 2022/23 and 2023/24: Eligibility limited to publication within the specific time frame. The prize will be a Tsh. 500,000 each.

B. NBS Prize

An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands in the master of official statistics.

C. OCGS Prize

An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands in the master of official statistics

D. Data Vission Prize

An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands in the Bachelor of official, Bachelor of data science, Bachelor of agriculture statistics and Bachelor of Business statistics.
